Hajime no Ippo is a Japanese boxing-themed manga series written and illustrated by George Morikawa.

HAJIME NO IPP/ THE FIGHTING SPIRIT

2000-2014

3 SEASONS

127 EPISODES and still counting

It follows the story of high school student Makunouchi Ippo, as he begins his career in boxing and over time obtains many titles and defeats various opponents.
