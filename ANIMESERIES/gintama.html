Gin Tama is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hideaki Sorachi

GINTAMA

2006-2021

10 SEASONS

369 EPISODES

The Amanto, aliens from outer space, have invaded Earth and taken over feudal Japan. As a result, a prohibition on swords has been established, and the samurai of Japan are treated with disregard as a consequence. However one man, Gintoki Sakata, still possesses the heart of the samurai.
